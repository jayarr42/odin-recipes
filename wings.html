Rollock Wings

Delicious Soy Glaze Wings

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken wings
  • 2/3 cup soy
  • 2/3 cup water
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp pepper
  • 6 tbsp sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Separate your wings (if not purchased pre-separated
  3. Heat soy, water, garlic powser, pepper, and sugar in a small sauce pan
  4. Gently stir ingredients until they combine. Note: Make sure that it does not boil
  5. Add the wings to a glass baking dish and cover with the sauce
  6. Cover wings with tin foil
  7. Cook covered for 30 minutes, then uncover and flip<. Do this two times.
  8. On your third flip, remove foil and replace in the oven for 30 minutes
  9. At this point, you may remove them from the oven, but the longer you cook and flip, the better. I've gone up to 3 hours before and it was great.
