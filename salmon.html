Rollock Family Salmon

A truly excellent salmon recipe, a family favorite, and incredibly simple

Some delicious salmon. Yum!

Making this delicious salmon is a breeze. It's the perfect meal to end the day with, partly because it takes no effort at all! We'll start with ingredients.

  • 2 filets of salmon
  • Large plastic zipper bag
  • Soy sauce
  • Lime Juice
  • Dill

Prep

  1. Start by patting dry the salmon
  2. Add soy sauce into the bag, enough that it will cover the salmon. Note: this will probably be less soy than you think!
  3. Add lime juice to the bag. Note: this is less of a science and more of an art. Add enough to turn the soy from black to brown color.
  4. Marinate the salmon in this mixture for a minimum of 30 minutes in the fridge. Note: some people say a short marinade is required for fish, I have not found this to be the case. Letting it sit for up to 2 hours has been fine and only improved things.
  5. When you are ready to cook it, remove from the fridge, pat dry, and let reach approximately room temp

Cooking

  1. reheat the oven to 425
  2. Preheat a skillet to medium heat
  3. Oil your pan
  4. Place salmon skin side up in the skillet and cook for 4-4.5 minutes
  5. Place the skillet in the oven for 4-4.5 minutes

Enjoy!
